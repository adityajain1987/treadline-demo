For the distributor's team

Distributor dashboard

Cheque & credit control: track every dealer's balance and credit limit, post-dated cheques, due-date reminders, and bounced-cheque alerts that auto-block a dealer.

Dealer ordering app

Orders arrive as WhatsApp messages, Arabic voice notes or PDF purchase orders — the AI reads them, matches each line to a tyre, and drafts a sales order in Odoo for one-click confirmation.
